safesoundr Privacy Policy

This app is designed to respect your privacy.

Local Data & Identity

Reporter details (Name, full address, phone number) are stored strictly on your device. safesoundr does not collect or store your personal identity. Audio recordings are also stored exclusively on your device.

Anonymous Cloud Mapping

To support noise pattern analysis, community mapping, and environmental noise trend heat-maps, safesoundr shares anonymous observation details to our secure cloud database. This data is fully aggregated and cannot be traced back to your individual identity. The synchronised data includes:

  • Exact location (Latitude and Longitude)
  • Anonymous User Identifier (UUID)
  • Date and time of incident
  • Noise type and severity score

This data is used strictly for non-PII analysis and does not include:

  • Your Name, House Number, or Email
  • Your Phone Number
  • Your personal notes or audio recordings

Purpose

Anonymous data may be used to analyse general patterns of noise activity.

Public Holiday Detection

Public holiday detection is based on third-party data from Nager.Date. Accuracy is not guaranteed and users should verify local regulations independently.

No Personal Data Collection

safesoundr does not require an account, email login, or name to function. We identify distinct reporting sources via a randomly generated ID that cannot be traced back to your personal identity.

Reporter Details (Local Only)

Information you enter into the "Reporter Details" section (Name, Address, Phone) is stored exclusively on your device. This data is used only to populate your professional observation reports and is never transmitted to safesoundr or any third parties.

Pro Upgrade & Billing

The PRO upgrade is an annual auto-renewable subscription. Subscriptions are billed yearly and automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Payment processing is handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. safesoundr does not collect or store your credit card details or financial information.

Audio Observations

Audio recordings are stored locally on your device's internal storage. They are never uploaded to our servers. You remain the sole owner and controller of all audio evidence recorded within the app.

Authority Submission

safesoundr provides a structured reporting tool for your convenience. The app does not automatically submit reports to any local authority or government body. Any submission of data to third parties is initiated and controlled entirely by you.

Disclaimer & Liability

safesoundr is an observational logging tool, not a certified acoustic measurement device. The app creator is not liable for any actions, disputes, or legal outcomes resulting from your use of the app or the sharing of reports generated by it.

Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact us at safesoundr@tenr.je.
